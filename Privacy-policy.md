# Privacy Policy – Unclickbait-VN

*Last Updated: May 2026*

Unclickbait-VN is committed to your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data to provide and improve the extension. **We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).**

## 1. What We Collect and Why

To provide the core functionality of rewriting clickbait titles, and to understand how the extension is used so we can improve it, we collect the following anonymous data:

### Core Functionality Data
- **Article Titles & URLs:** When you browse supported sites (Kenh14.vn, Dantri.com.vn, VnExpress.net, 24h.com.vn, Tuoitre.vn), the extension sends article titles and URLs to our remote API (`https://api.sharingisfun.io.vn`) for processing.

### Anonymous Usage & Telemetry Data
If telemetry is enabled (default), we collect usage data to help us improve the product. This data is fully anonymous and includes:
- **Device ID & Session ID:** A random UUID (Universally Unique Identifier) is generated to measure retention and session length. It cannot be linked to your identity or personal accounts.
- **Product Usage Metrics:** The number of titles processed, changed, and toggled, as well as API latency and cache hit rates. Article IDs are hashed (SHA-256) before transmission to ensure your reading habits remain private.
- **Engagement Signals:** Scroll depth, dwell time on rewritten titles, and extension popup interactions.
- **Environment Info:** Browser name/version, OS platform, screen resolution, and extension version.

### What We Do NOT Collect
- Personal details (name, email, address, etc.)
- Browsing history outside of the 5 supported news websites
- Keystrokes, passwords, or cookies
- The body content of the articles you read

## 2. Telemetry Opt-Out

We believe in user control. You can disable the collection of anonymous usage and telemetry data at any time:
1. Click the Unclickbait extension icon in your browser toolbar.
2. Toggle the **"Send usage data"** switch to the OFF position.

When opted out, the extension will function normally but will not send any background telemetry events to our servers.

## 3. Data Storage and Retention

- **Local Storage:** The extension stores settings (like your toggle preferences), cache, and your anonymous Device ID locally in your browser.
- **Server Storage:** Anonymous telemetry data sent to our servers is stored securely. We retain this telemetry data for up to **90 days** for product analytics, after which it is aggregated or deleted.
- Processed article titles are not linked to your Device ID on our servers.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties. Data is strictly used internally to maintain and improve the Unclickbait-VN extension.

## 5. Feedback and Uninstall Survey

If you choose to uninstall the extension, you may be redirected to an optional feedback survey. Any information provided in this survey is voluntary and used solely to understand why you left and how we can improve.

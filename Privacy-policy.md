## Privacy Policy – Unclickbait-VN

Unclickbait-VN does not collect personally identifiable information.

### Data Usage

This extension processes article titles and URLs from kenh14.vn to generate neutral, informative alternatives. This data may be sent to a remote API (https://api.sharingisfun.io.vn) for processing.

### Data Storage

No personal data is stored by the extension. Processed content is not linked to any individual user.

### Data Sharing

No user data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

### Purpose

All data processing is strictly used to provide the core functionality of rewriting clickbait titles.
